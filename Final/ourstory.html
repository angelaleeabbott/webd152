Our Story

Our story begins 7 years ago when John and Sarah decided to leave the corporate world and pursue their dream of creating a unique resort experience. Drawing inspiration from their travels, they envisioned a place where guests could escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the beauty of nature. With hard work, determination, and a little bit of luck, their dream became a reality.


Today, our family-owned yurt resort is a beloved destination for travelers seeking an unforgettable glamping experience. With a focus on sustainability, comfort, and personalized service, our resort has become a true labor of love. We invite you to join us on this journey and experience the magic of our resort for yourself.

"An experience my family and I will never forget." Jessica M.

Creation

At our resort, we believe in the values of sustainability, nature, and community. We are committed to running our business in an eco-friendly and sustainable manner, using green building practices and minimizing our impact on the environment. We also believe in the healing power of nature and strive to create an environment that allows our guests to connect with the natural world and find peace and rejuvenation. Finally, we believe in the importance of community, both within our resort and in the wider world. We aim to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere where all guests feel valued and supported. These values are at the heart of everything we do, from the design of our yurts to the activities we offer and the way we interact with our guests.
